Brigadeiro Recipe

Here is a Brazilian brigadeiro recipe written by Lu√≠sa Michel! Ingredients: Condensed Milk, Butter, Powdered Chocolate

Steps: 1. In a pan, stir the condensed milk, butter and powdered chocolate. For every 250 ml of condensed milk, use two spoons of butter and two spoons of powdered milk. 2. Cook with a light flame. Keep mixing until a creamy consistent is reached. 3. Wait to cool and enjoy!

You can also roll them into balls. For that, it is better to let them refrigerate for a couple of hours and then form balls with your hands. You can add sprinkles to finish.
